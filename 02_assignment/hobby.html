Why I Love Bible Quizzing

Bible Quizzing is more than just a competition; it is a powerful way to hide God’s Word in my heart.

By memorizing specific books of the Bible, I am able to reflect on Scripture throughout the day, finding guidanceand strength in every verse.

Benefits of Bible Quizzing

  • Value God’s Word
  • Build Character
  • Work Together
  • Life Lessons
  • Make Friends
